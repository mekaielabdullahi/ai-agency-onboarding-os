## Pro Tips

1. **Always collect baseline metrics upfront**
   - Section 3 of intake form is critical
   - You can't prove ROI without a baseline

2. **Calculate estimated ROI before kickoff**
   - Shows client the potential value
   - Sets clear success metrics
   - Justifies your pricing

3. **Track real metrics at 30/60/90 days**
   - Compare to baseline
   - Demonstrate actual ROI
   - Build case studies

4. **Use the data for sales**
   - "Our average client saves $X/month"
   - "Typical ROI is XXX% in 90 days"
   - Build library of case studies

5. **Follow the workflow exactly**
   - It's been proven in production
   - Don't skip steps
   - Use the checklists
